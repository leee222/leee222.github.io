GRACE E. LEE

Welcome to my website!

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, supervised by Dr. Aixin Sun. I received B.S. and M.S. in KAIST, South Korea, in 2014 and 2016, respectively.

My research interests are text mining, information retrieval, and natural language processing. Currently I am working on biomedical document ranking for systematic reviews.
