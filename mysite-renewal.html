Grace E. Lee

I am a Ph.D. candidate in School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. I received B.S. and M.S. in KAIST, South Korea, in 2014 and 2016 respectively. My research interests are text mining, information retrieval, and natural language processing. I am currently working on biomedical document ranking for systematic reviews in evidence-based medicine.
